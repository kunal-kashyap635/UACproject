1. Big Picture

Socho ek pipeline hai jisme bachche move karte hain:

1️⃣ CBP → bachche pakadti hai
2️⃣ HHS → unko care, shelter, treatment deta hai
3️⃣ Sponsor → bachche discharge ho jaate hain

👉 Problem ye hai:

Kitne bachche aa rahe?
Kitne system ke andar phase hue?
Kitne nikal rahe?
System overload to nahi ho raha?

hme exact yahi analytics banana wala hai.

2. Actual Goal (Simple Words)

Tu ML model nahi bana raha abhi ❌
Tu SYSTEM HEALTH MONITOR bana raha hai ✅

Matlab:
System overloaded hai ya nahi?
Future mein overload hone ke chances?
Kab relief milta hai?
Kab pressure zyada hota hai?
